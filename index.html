About Anil Thomas

Anil Thomas is an experienced Aviation Engineering professional. He has about 20 years of working experience on A380 APUs and Rolls Royce Trent 900 Engine. According to Anil Thomas’ point of view, change is the core factor that drives an individual towards upward progression in life and it has to be generated by ourselves through effective and suitable methods.

‘The world we have created is a product of our thinking; it cannot be changed without changing our thinking’ says Anil from his experience.

Anil Thomas is zealous to read, speak and learn which makes him an eloquent orator. His peer and professional coaching programs gained him a mettle in speed reading, conversation tactics, rational thinking, and social intelligence.

He coordinates and cooperates various radiant online intellectual clubs including E-Bookery, Young Empowerment, and Entrepreneurs’ Connect. At present he is leading a new age networking and learning platform, BRAINCITI, co-founded by him.
