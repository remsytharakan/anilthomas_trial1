E-Bookery: Read Through Life

In a chaotic information overloaded environment, reading a book while tucked up in your bed or sitting under a tree or in a quiet library is no less than meditation! Have you ever considered the fact that in most cases a book is more impactful than a movie based on that book? The reason is that while reading a book every person interprets and imagines things as they see them, whereas in a movie everything that you see is entirely the director’s interpretation! Every dark street, or alley, or tree described in a story is owned by you and only you see it that way – which is uniquely different. Presenting you ‘E Bookery International’ - a reading group specifically for readers who read through life, intending to bridge the global distance and share literature through storytelling. With an aim to promote a love of literature; learn and discuss something that matters to them, E Bookery International invites participants to bring up suggestions, formulate opinions, and discuss stories from a wide array of genres. This promotes a growth mindset and gives the participants a chance to discover what interests them the most.

Once a week, our participants get an opportunity to analyze the content of any book in form of book reviews, form their thoughts, and present them in public as storytelling. The point of view of every participant constitutes a small window through which catching a glimpse of their personalities and thought processes are made possible. With a demographically diverse audience, participants are encouraged to push themselves and grow as readers and learners through our practice, something that can be applied to other areas of their day-to-day lives as well. Two voluntary speakers every week will be sharing or reviewing any literature of their choice for twenty minutes each. With an aim to take you on a journey from being a member of E Bookery International to providing you a chance to publish your book – we wish to expand and grow into a community of avid readers and leaders.
